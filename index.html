Our Services

  • Wash & Iron / Steam Ironing: Garments are washed and then pressed to remove wrinkles
  • Stain Removal: Specialized treatments for stubborn stains on various types of fabric.
  • Express/Same-day Service: Option to have laundry processed in a short turnaround, usually 24-48 hours or even same day for an extra charge.
  • Pickup & Delivery: Convenience of home pickup and drop-off for laundry orders, often offered free within limited radius.

  • Darning (Raffu) & Color Dyeing: Repair for torn garments and fabric color restoration.
